# UX & Design Principles - Hush Gentle

## Brand Identity
- **Keywords**: Organic, Gentle, Honest, Calm, Premium.
- **Aesthetic**: Minimalist, "Clean Girl" aesthetic, High-end Spa.
- **Core Experience**: Not transactional; relational. We want the user to feel soothed, not pressured.

## Design System

### Color Palette (Pastel & Neutral)
- **Primary**: `#5B6C5D` (Sage Green) - Trust, Nature.
- **Secondary**: `#D8C3A5` (Warm Beige) - Skin, Softness.
- **Background**: `#F9F7F2` (Off-white/Eggshell) - Not harsh white.
- **Text**: `#2C2C2C` (Soft Black) - Readable but not stark.
- **Accent**: `#E8B4B8` (Muted Rose) - Gentle highlights.

### Typography
- **Headings**: `Playfair Display` or `Fraunces` (Serif) - Premium, establish authority.
- **Body**: `Inter` or `Lato` (Sans-serif) - Clean, readable, functional.

### Spacing & Layout
- **Grid**: 12-column grid with generous gutters (24px+).
- **Whitespace**: Extensive use of whitespace to let products "breathe".
- **Radius**: `rounded-2xl` strictly; no sharp edges.

## Component Patterns

### Product Cards
- No borders, just subtle shadow on hover.
- Image takes up 70% of the card.
- "Add to Cart" is always visible but secondary style until hover.

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Solid Sage Green, white text, pill-shaped.
- **Secondary**: Outline Sage Green, transparent bg.

### Chatbot UI
- **Avatar**: Friendly illustration, not a robot icon.
- **Animation**: Smooth slide-in from bottom right.
- **Tone**: "How can I help you gently today?"

## Accessibility (A11y)
- **Contrast**: Ensure text passes WCAG AA.
- **Focus**: Visible focus rings for keyboard navigation.
- **Images**: All product images **must** have descriptive `alt` text.
